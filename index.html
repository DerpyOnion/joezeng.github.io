This page is an archive of all the games I posted that got popular here.
I don't really post new games here anymore. Most of my projects are on GitLab now.

  • Ralsei Hwrot January 2019

    With the release of Deltarune... came one part of the game that I just had to make into its own thing. YEET!

    •

  • Unfair Undyne February 2016

    My most-played game to date. Everyone liked Endless Sans so much that I made an Undyne version too! It's way better made and also much harder.

    •

  • Endless Sans October 2015

    A game with (a few of) the attacks from Sans from Undertale, packaged into an endless version reminiscent of the Super Gravitron from VVVVVV.

    •

  • Goomy Clicker 2 October 2014

    A sequel to the original Goomy Clicker.

    I made this new version because the old one was incomplete and I wanted to make something that had responsive design. It ended up being incomplete anyway.

    •

  • 144 August 2014

    A version of 2048 where any number of equal tiles will match together, and the goal is to make 144. It's harder than it looks.

    •

  • Goomy Clicker October 2013

    The first game I ever made that went viral. Back in a simpler time when Cookie Clicker had just come out and nobody had the idea to make clones of it yet.

    I originally released this game as a "v0.01 pre-alpha" as a joke, but because it went viral even in its "not even really a game yet" state, it prompted many people to make their own low-effort clones.

    •
